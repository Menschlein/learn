speaking of pixels

raster images also contain a

pixel density

otherwise known as pixels per inch or ppi

common values are: 72, 150, 225, & 300

sometimes people call this dpi, or dots per inch

this is not always correct

dpi refers to the physical density of dots of the image when printed

ppi refers to intended size of the image when printed

you will almost never use dpi
