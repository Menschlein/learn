SPEED

painting

When we speed paint

we aren’t just using actual digital layers

layering areas of color

over and over and over and...

And we do it

as quickly as we can

We’re not looking for

picture perfect

If anything, speed painting is

a mixture

Photorealism & Impressionism

Speed painting has four general steps

Evaluation

see what you have and pick the least refined area of your painting

Zooming

make the spot you picked your canvas and forget the rest

Shapeshift

changing your brush and color to match your needs

A general rule of thumb for shapeshifting:

smaller

as you zoom in

larger

as you zoom out

The brush radius property defines the

size of the brush

The brush hardness property defines the

hardness of the edge on a brush

The brush opacity property defines the

transparency of the brush stroke

Blocking

placing areas of color to create a general idea of the final product

(drop color areas like a boss!)

re-evaluate and repeat
