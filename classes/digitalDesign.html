Introduction all digital all the time

Intent

This course is designed to introduce the basics of digital art and design: raster and vector graphics, per-pixel vs photo manipulation, digital painting, typography, and text flow.

Structure

This class includes lectures, demonstrations, and studio activities. Understanding will be measured through scheduled projects, and will focus on application of knowledge rather than reiteration.

Critiques

Each project will take a balanced approach of large, small, and individual critiques, as best fits the nature of that particular project.
